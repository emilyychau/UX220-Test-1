UX220 Test 1

This summative assessment is assigned to all students enrolled in the UX220BR class - Web Design Lab I. It is designed to draw your attention to the basics of html and css. It shall be written on Wednesday, October 25th 2023 starting at 7pm EST and is meant to be 90 minutes long. It comprises three questions that cover the basics of CSS styles, setting the document fonts through Google Fonts, and clickable image maps.
