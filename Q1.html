Tim Berners Lee


Tim Berners Lee was born in London, England on June 8th 1955. He is known as the inventor of the web, but he also founded/invented many other things, like the World Wide Web and the coding language of HTML. He's an honorarily on the board of Directors of the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C), an organization which he founded in 1994.
