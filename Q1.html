Tim Berners Lee


Tim Berners-Lee was born in London, England on June 8th 1955 (Dennis, 2023). At CERN, he invented what we know as the world-wide web; he also founded/invented many other things like the the markup coding language of HTML (the language that I'm using to write this very assessment!). He's honorarily on the board of Directors of the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C), an organization dedicated to creating guidelines and standards that help make the internet and the web more accessible to all.

More information about the life and work of Sir Berners-Lee can be found through the W3C website.

  Dennis, M. Aaron (2023, August 8). Tim Berners-Lee. Encyclopedia Britannica. https://www.britannica.com/biography/Tim-Berners-Lee
